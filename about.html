TASL is a Robust and dynamic consultancy firm operating in Kenya. With an array of a well-trained and informed team, TASL has mastered the art of combining talent, teamwork, and experience and acquired knowledge pertinent to providing solutions to our clients’ needs.

We proclaim in-depth understanding of the rapidly changing business environment and the need for organizations, be it Governmental, Private Businesses or Non-Governmental to tailor their operations, structures, cultures, policies, goals and activities to suit the desires of their clients.

In recognition of the fact that clients constitute any organization’s lifeline, TASL is committed to serving them diligently and with utmost efficiency.

Concurrently TASL has overly ensured that our core values of Integrity, Passion, Teamwork Leadership and Commitment to Change, Results and Recognition, become the foundation upon which our services are rendered. This is aimed at helping our clients make improvements to their operations, performance and cultures thereby fostering the realization of their set goals and equipping them with the required skills and techniques to meet their customers’ needs.
